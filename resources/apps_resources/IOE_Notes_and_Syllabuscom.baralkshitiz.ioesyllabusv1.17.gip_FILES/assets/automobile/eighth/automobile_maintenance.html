Course objectives

Course outline

  1. Hygiene and safety
    (6 hours)
    1. Introduction to hygiene and safety
    2. Workshop environment: health hazards and prevention, sanitation, ventilation, heating system and instrumentation
    3. Chemical handling, fire protection, electrical accident and prevention
    4. Protection from noise and air pollution
    5. Safety requirements for workshop equipment and operations
  2. Concept of maintenance and repair
    (6 hours)
    1. Maintenance: introduction, types, objectives
    2. Preventive maintenance: introduction, objectives, elements and advantages
    3. Scheduled maintenance: introduction and advantages
    4. Break-down and corrective maintenance: introduction, advantages and limitations
    5. Predictive maintenance
    6. Maintenance costs
    7. Total productive maintenance (TPM)
    8. Repair: introduction and its types
  3. Wear in vehicles
    (3 hours)
    1. Natural and accidental wear
    2. Types of wear: adhesive, oxidative, thermal, abrasive, pitting wear
    3. Prevention of wear
    4. Repair of wear: welding, brazing, metal spray, machining, boring, honing
    5. Replacement of parts
  4. Measuring instrument and testing equipment
    (6 hours)
    1. Measuring instrument: Vernier caliper, micrometer, dial gauge, bore gauge, feeler gauge, plastic gauge, compression gauge, multimeter, oscilloscope, pressure gauge, hydrometer, thermometer, vibration transducer, decibel meter, speedometer, tachometer
    2. Testing equipment: engine scanner, engine analyzer, engine dynamometer, chassis dynamometer, timing light, injector tester, exhaust gas analyzer, spark plug tester, battery tester, fuel injection pump (FIP) test bench, wheel balancer, wheel alignment tester
  5. Service stations
    (6 hours)
    1. Types of service stations: highway, small, medium, big and special service station
    2. Functions, operations and activities of service stations
    3. Workshop layout for service stations
    4. Testing or diagnostic procedure
    5. Overhauling process: engine and transmission
    6. Machines used in servicing and lubrication: washing machine, grease gun, oil cane, oil filter wrench, air compressor
    7. Denting and painting
    8. Hand tools and equipment for service stations
  6. Workshop management practice
    (3 hours)
    1. Workshop documents and records: pre-delivery inspection, job cards, bill, satisfaction voucher, history sheet, warranty policy
    2. Customer complaints and handling
