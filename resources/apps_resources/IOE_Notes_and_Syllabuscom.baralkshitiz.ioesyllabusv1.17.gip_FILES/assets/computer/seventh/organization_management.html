ORGANIZATION AND MANAGEMENT
ME 708

Lecture   : 3
Tutorial   : 2
Practical : 0

Course Objective:

To give knowledge about organizational management and internal organization of companies required for managing an enterprise. Also to make familiar with personnel management, case study, management information syitem motivation and leadership for developing managerial skills.
