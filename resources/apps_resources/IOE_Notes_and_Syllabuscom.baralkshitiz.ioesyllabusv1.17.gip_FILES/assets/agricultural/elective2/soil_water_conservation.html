DESIGN OF SOIL & WATER CONSERVATION STRUCTURES
AE 765 02

Lecture   : 3
Tutorial   : 1
Practical : 3/2

Course Objective:

The primary objective of this course is to familiarize the students with the various soil and water conservation structures and their design that exercises in Agricultural Engineering. Secondly this course emphasizes in design and development of such simple and effective technologies and structures that conserve soil and water.

  1. Introduction
    (3 hours)
    1. Importance of structural means in soil and water Conservation
    2. Functional requirements of soil and water conservation Structures
    3. Classification of soil and water conservation structures
  2. Vegetated Waterways
    (4 hours)
    1. Types of waterways
    2. Factors affecting shape of waterways
    3. Design considerations in vegetated waterways
    4. Layout, construction and maintenance of waterways
  3. Design of Runoff Measuring Structures
    (4 hours)
    1. Weirs and Noteches
    2. Par shall flume
    3. H‐ flume
    4. Cut‐ throat flume
  4. Design of Spillways
    (12 hours)
    1. Drop Spillway:
      1. General description and function
      2. Components of drop Spillway
      3. Hydrologic and hydraulic design
      4. Freeboard and acration of weirs
      5. Structural design of drop Spillway:
        1. Loads on headwalls
        2. Variable affecting Equivalent Fluid Pressure
        3. Determination of saturation line
        4. Triangular load diagram for various flow conditions
        5. Uplift pressure estimation
        6. Safety against sliding, overturning, crushing and tension
    2. Chute Spillway:
      1. General description and function
      2. Components of Chute Spillway
      3. Hydrologic and Hydraulic Design
      4. Energy Dissipators
      5. Structural design of chute Spillway:
        1. Load on headwalls
        2. Uplift pressure estimation
        3. Safety against sliding, overturning crushing and tension
      6. Design of SAF Stilling basin
    3. Drop Inlet Spillway
      1. General description and function
      2. Components of drop inlet Spillway
      3. Hydrologic and hydraulic design
      4. Structural design
  5. Stream Bank Erosion Control
    (6 hours)
    1. Vegetative measures for stream bank stabilization
    2. Slope protection:
      1. Soding and Turfing
      2. Stone revetment
      3. Gabion revetment
      4. Brushwood rollers
    3. Indirect protection works
      1. Types and function of retards
      2. Classification and functions of spurs
      3. Design of spurs
  6. Design of Landslide Controlling Structures
    (6 hours)
    1. Mechanics of landslide
    2. landslide control measures
      1. Slope treatment
      2. Surface and sub‐ surface drainage
      3. Retaining walls:
        1. Types of retaining walls
        2. Design of cantilever retaining walls
        3. Design of counter fort type retaining walls
  7. Check Dams
    (4 hours)
    1. General description and function
    2. Types of check dams
    3. Design of check dams
    4. Stability analysis of check dams
  8. Farm Ponds and Reservoirs
    (6 hours)
    1. General description and function
    2. Components of farm ponds and reservoirs
    3. Site selection
    4. Design of embankment: type, foundation condition, Cross‐section, Free board and wave Protection
    5. Seepage through embankment
    6. Spillway and Spillway location
