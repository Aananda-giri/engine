SOLAR PHOTO‐VOLTAIC SYSTEM AND APPLICATION
AE 765 04

Lecture   : 3
Tutorial   : 1
Practical : 3/2

Course Objective:

The primary objective of this course is to introduce solar photo‐voltaic system and their conversion technology used to fulfil the requirement of energy in Agricultural Engineering activities in rural areas. Secondly this course emphasizes in design and development of such simple and effective technologies that converts solar energy into usable form and supply it to operate tools, equipments and machineries used in agricultural works.

  1. Fundamentals of Solar Photo‐Voltaic System
    (10 hours)
    1. Principle of Photo‐Voltaic Energy Conversion:
      1. Review of Electrical Power System
      2. Principle of photo‐Voltaic effect
      3. Types of Solar cells
      4. Spectral response of Solar cell materials
      5. Electrical parameters of Solar cells
      6. Effect of environment on performance of Solar cells
    2. Solar Modules and Arrays:
      1. Solar cells modules
      2. IV Curve for a module
      3. Blocking Diode and bypass Diode
      4. Solar arrays‐ role of installation Diode
  2. Components of Solar Photo‐Voltaic System
    (10 hours)
    1. Batteries
      1. Types of batteries
      2. Battery terminology and performance characteristics
      3. Chemistry of lead acid battery
      4. Battery Test and Maintenance
    2. Charge Regulators
      1. Principle of operation
      2. Parameters of charge regulator
      3. Methods of battery charging through charge regulator
    3. DC‐AC Inverters
      1. Basic principles of an inverter
      2. Parameters of an inverter
      3. Types of inverter
    4. DC‐ DC Converter
      1. Principle of DC‐DC converter
      2. Parameters of DC‐DC converters
    5. Lamps and Non‐luminous Loads to Solar PV System
      1. Basic lamp types of Solar PV System
      2. Other loads to Solar PV system
  3. Design of lighting with Solar PV system
    (10 hours)
    1. Load Calculation
      1. Load characteristics‐AC or DC, Apparent or Real,
      2. Critical and Non‐critical
      3. Annual loading profile
      4. Maximum load and current
      5. Nominal system and array voltage
    2. Module and Array Sizing
    3. Battery Sizing
    4. Sizing of Charge Regulator
    5. Cable Sizing
    6. Inverter Sizing
    7. DC‐AC Converter Sizing
    8. Sizing of luminous and non‐luminous loads
  4. Design of Solar PV Water Pumping System
    (10 hours)
    1. Solar PV water pumping system configurations
      1. Appraisal of Solar PV water pumping for drinking
      2. water supply and Irrigation
      3. System components
      4. Selection of pumps
      5. Selections of motors
      6. Configuration of Solar arrays and panels
      7. Maximum power point tracking circuitry
    2. Array wiring and mounting of water pumps
      1. Array wiring
      2. Array Mounting
      3. Considerations for tracking support structures
    3. Water pumping system design
      1. Array sizing
      2. Selection of pump form manufacturer’s data
      3. Installation diagram
      4. General algorithm for system design
  5. Solar PV Technology in Nepal
    (5 hours)
    1. Scope and potential of Solar PV system in Nepal
    2. Agencies involved in development and promotion of Solar PV system
    3. Policies and program for promotion of Solar PV system
    4. Nepal PV quality assurance‐ conditions of NIPQA‐ 2000(2002)
