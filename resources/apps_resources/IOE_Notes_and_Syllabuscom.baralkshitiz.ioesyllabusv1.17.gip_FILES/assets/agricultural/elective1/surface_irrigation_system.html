PLANNING AND DESIGN OF SURFACE IRRIGATION SYSTEM
AE 725 01

Lecture   : 3
Tutorial   : 1
Practical : 3/2

Course Objective:

To familiarize and orient the students in theory, planning, technological development and management in engineering problem solving in the area of surface irrigation system.

  1. Introduction
    (2 hours)
    1. Classification of Irrigation System
    2. Comparison of Irrigation System and Their Relative Merits
    3. Selection of Irrigation System
  2. Surface Irrigation System
    (5 hours)
    1. Estimation of Water Requirement of Selected Command Area
    2. Delta, Base Period, Duty and Their Relationship
    3. Factors Affecting Canal Duty
    4. Classification of Canals and Canal Network
    5. Nomenclature and Terminology Used in Canal Distribution System
  3. Planning Canal Irrigation System
    (3 hours)
    1. Considerations in Canal Alignment
    2. Curves in Canals
    3. Optimal Layout of Canal Network
    4. L‐Section in Canal Network
  4. Design of Irrigation Channels
    (7 hours)
    1. Design Based on Maximum Permissible Velocity
    2. Kennedy's Silt Theory and Kennedy's Method of Channel Design
    3. Silt Carrying Capacity by Kennedy's Theory
    4. Limitations of Kennedy's Theory
    5. Lacey's Regime Theory and Shock Concept
    6. Lacey's Theory Applied to Channel Design
    7. Comparison of Lacey's and Kennedy's Theory
    8. Garret's and Lacey's Diagram Applied to Channel Design
      1. Components of Canal Cross‐Section: Side Slope, Berm,
        1. Free Board, Bank Width, Barrow Pit, Spoil Bank
    9. Lining of Irrigation Channels, Types of Lining,
      1. Comparison of Different Lining Materials
    10. Design of Lined Canals
    11. Maintenance of Irrigation Channels
  5. Underground Pipe Line Conveyance
    (7 hours)
    1. Purpose and Advantage
    2. Materials of Underground Pipe Line and their Properties
    3. Design of Underground Pipeline:
      1. Design Velocities
      2. Pipe Diameter
      3. Frictional Head Loss
      4. Design of Pump Stand
      5. Surge and Water Hammer Protection
      6. Air Vents
    4. Ancillary Structures and Devices in Underground Pipeline
    5. Installation of Underground Pipeline
  6. Hydraulic Structures in Surface Irrigation System
    (12 hours)
    1. Headworks:
      1. Components of Headworks
      2. Types of Diversion Works
      3. Types of Weirs and their Location
      4. Consideration in Weir Design
    2. Head and Cross Regulators:
      1. Purpose and Location of Head and Cross Regulators
      2. Design Considerations
    3. Sediment Control and Exclusion Devices:
      1. Sediment Control and Exclusion at Headworks
      2. Design Criteria for Sediment Excluders
    4. Canal Drops and Drop Structures:
      1. Types of Drops and Their Selection
      2. Design Principle of Drop Structures
    5. Cross‐Drainage Works:
      1. Purpose and Types of Cross‐Drainage Works
      2. Selection of Cross Drainage Works
      3. Design Considerations
    6. Canal Outlets:
      1. Types‐ Modular, Semi‐Modular and Non‐Modular with their Salient Features
      2. Parameters Governing Behaviour of outlets
      3. Design Consideration in Canal Outlet
  7. Land Development
    (7 hours)
    1. Purpose and Importance of Land Development in Command Area
    2. Land Grading and Farm Layout
    3. Consolidation of Land Holding and Its Importance
    4. Conjunctive Use‐ Concept and Scope
    5. Integrated Development of Command Area
  8. Preparation of Project Plan for Command Area
    (2 hours)
