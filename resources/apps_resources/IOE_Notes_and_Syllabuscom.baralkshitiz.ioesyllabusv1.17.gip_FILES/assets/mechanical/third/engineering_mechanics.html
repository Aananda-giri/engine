ENGINEERING MECHANICS
ME 502

Lecture   : 3
Tutorial   : 1
Practical : 0

Course Objective:

To provide the fundamental principles, concepts and application of mechanics for solving engineering problems. To become familiar with the analytical/graphical methods for solving problems of mechanics, mainly of dynamics.

  1. Virtual Work
    (2 hours)
    1. Definition of Work and Virtual Work
    2. Principal of Virtual Work for a Particle and a Rigid Body
    3. Uses of the Principal of Virtual Work
    4. Virtual Work Done by Moments
  2. Kinetics of Particles: Force, Mass and Acceleration
    (6 hours)
    1. Newton’s Second Law of Motion
    2. Consistent System of Units
    3. Equations of Motion: Radial and Transverse Components
    4. Dynamic Equilibrium: Inertia Force
    5. Principle of Motion of the Mass Centre
    6. Motion due to a Central Force, Conservation of Momentum
    7. Newton’s Law of Gravitation
  3. Kinetics of Particles: Work Energy Principles
    (4 hours)
    1. Work Done by a Force
    2. Kinetic Energy of a Particle
    3. Principle of Work and Energy, Applications
    4. Power and Efficiency
    5. Potential Energy
    6. Conservation of Energy
  4. Kinetics of Particles: Impulse and Momentum
    (6 hours)
    1. Principle of Impulse and Momentum
    2. Impulsive Motion and Impact
    3. Direct Central Impact
    4. Oblique Central Impact
  5. Kinematics of Rigid Bodies
    (7 hours)
    1. Introduction to Plane Kinematics of Rigid Bodies
    2. Translation, Rotation and General Plane Motion
    3. Absolute and Relative Velocity in Plane Motion
    4. Instantaneous Centre of Rotation
    5. Absolute and Relative Acceleration in Plane Motion
    6. Motion Relative to Rotating Axis; Coriolis Acceleration
  6. Plane Kinetics of Rigid Bodies: Force, Mass and Acceleration
    (8 hours)
    1. Mass Moment of Inertia
      1. Moment of Inertia of Mass
      2. Radius of Gyration
      3. Parallel Axis Theorem
      4. Mass moment of inertia of Composite Bodies
    2. Force and Acceleration
      1. Equations of Motion for a Rigid Body
      2. Angular Momentum of a Rigid Body in Plane Motion
      3. Plane Motion of a Rigid Body: D’Alembert’s Principle
      4. Application of Rigid Body Motion in the Plane
      5. Constrained Motion in the Plane
  7. Plane Motion of Rigid Bodies: Work and Energy Method
    (4 hours)
    1. Work Energy Relations
    2. Work of a Force on a Rigid Body
    3. Kinetic Energy of a Rigid Body
    4. Principle of Work and Energy for a Rigid Body
    5. Acceleration from Work Energy Method
  8. Plane Motion of Rigid Bodies: Impulse and Momentum Method
    (4 hours)
    1. Impulse and Momentum of a Rigid Body
    2. Conservation of Angular and Linear Momentum
    3. Impulsive Motion and Eccentric Impact of Rigid Bodies
  9. Lagrangian Dynamics
    (4 hours)
    1. Degree of Freedom in mechanical systems and Generalized Coordinates
    2. D’Alembert’s – Lagrange Principle and Lagrange Equations of motion
    3. Differential equation of motion for a system of particles
    4. Conservation Theorems
