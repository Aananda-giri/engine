DESIGN OF STEEL AND TIMBER STRUCTURES
CE 651

Lecture   : 4
Tutorial   : 2
Practical : 0

Course Objectives:

  • To make students capable to design ordinary steel and timber structures.
  • To prepare students for advanced knowledge on design of complex steel and timber structures.

PART A: STEEL STRUCTURES

  1. Steel Structures and their Analysis and Design
    (4 hours)
    1. Introduction to Steel Structures
    2. Structural Steel and Classification of Steel Sections
    3. Method of Analysis and Design
    4. Design Process and Basis for Design
  2. Working Stress Design Method
    (2hours)
    1. Basic Assumptions in Working Stress Design
    2. Service Load and Permissible Stresses
    3. Design in Tension, Compression, Bending and Shear
  3. Limit State Design Method
    (3 hours)
    1. Safety and Serviceability Requirements of Structure
    2. Different Limit States for Steel Design
    3. Design Strength of Materials and Design Loads
    4. Limit State of Strength
    5. Limit State of Serviceability
  4. Connections in Steel Structures
    (10 hours)
    1. Types of Connections
    2. Welded Connections
      1. Welds and Welding
      2. Design of Simple Welded Connections
      3. Design of Eccentric Welded Connections
    3. Bolted Connections
      1. Bolts and Bolting
      2. Design of Simple Bolted Connections
      3. Design of Eccentric Bolted Connections
    4. Introduction to Riveted Connection
  5. Tension Members
    (4hours)
    1. Types of Tension Member
    2. Sectional Area of Tension Members
    3. Design of Tension Members of Simple and Built-Up Sections
    4. Design of Lug Angles
    5. Tension Splices
  6. Compression Members
    (10 hours)
    1. Types of Compression Member
    2. Buckling Behavior of Column
    3. Design of Column of Simple and Built-Up Sections
    4. Design of Lateral Bracings of Compression Members
    5. Design of Eccentrically Loaded Columns
    6. Design of Column Bases
      1. Axially Loaded Column Bases
      2. Eccentrically Loaded Column Bases
    7. Design of Column Splices
  7. Flexure Members
    (13 hours)
    1. Types of Beams
    2. Design of Simple Beams
    3. Design of Built-Up Beams
    4. Design of Plate Girders
      1. Elements of a Plate Girder
      2. Preliminary Design
      3. Design for Bending, Shear, Deflection and Lateral Stability
      4. Curtailment of Flange Plates
      5. Design of Web and Flange Splices
  8. Design of Roof Trusses
    (4 hours)
    1. Types of Roof Truss and Components of Roof Truss
    2. Loads on Roof Truss
    3. Design of Roof Components


      PART B: TIMBER STRUCTURES

  9. Timber Structures and Design Methods
    (2 hours)
    1. Introduction to Timber Structures
    2. Structural Timber and Factors Affecting the Strength of Timber
    3. Design Methods and Basis for Design
  10. Joints in Timber Structures
    (2 hours)
    1. Types of Joints
    2. Design of Bolted Joints
    3. Design of Nailed Joints
  11. Design of Compression Members
    (3 hours)
    1. Types of TimberColumn
    2. Design of Timber Columns
    3. Introduction to Column Bases
  12. Design of Flexure Members
    (3 hours)
    1. Types of Beam
    2. Design of Timber and Flitched Beams

Course Project:

A Course project on integrated design of a building/industrial structure.
