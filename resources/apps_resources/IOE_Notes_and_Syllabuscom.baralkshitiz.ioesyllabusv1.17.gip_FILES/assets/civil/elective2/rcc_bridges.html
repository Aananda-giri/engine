DESIGN OF BRIDGES
CE 76502

Lecture   : 3
Tutorial   : 1
Practical : 3/2

Course Objectives:

  • Introduce bridge structures & their types and make capable to select appropriate bridge type
  • Make capable to analyze and design simple reinforced concrete and steel bridge deck, bridge bearing and substructure of bridge
  • Familiarize with the method of construction and maintenance of bridges
  1. Introduction to Bridge Structures and Fundamentals of Bridge Design
    (6 hours)
    1. Bridge and its components
    2. Types of bridges and their characteristics
    3. Selection of bridge type
    4. Essential design data and their acquisition
    5. General design requirements
  2. Bridge loading and Responses
    (4 hours)
    1. Bridge Loads
    2. Bridge Responses
  3. Bridge Deck Analysis and Method of Lateral load Distribution
    (6 hours)
    1. General principle and methods of bridge deck analysis
    2. Effective Width Method
    3. Courbon's Method
    4. Distribution CoefficientMethod
    5. Hendry Jaeger Method
    6. Longitudinal and lateral positioning of moving loads and response calculation
  4. Design of Simple Reinforced Concrete Bridge
    (5 hours)
    1. Design of RC Slab Bridge
    2. Design of RC T-Beam Bridge
  5. Design of Simple Steel Bridge
    (5 hours)
    1. Design of plate girder and composite bridge
    2. Design of truss bridge
  6. Design of Bridge Substructure
    (10 hours)
    1. Design of Pier
    2. Design of Abutment
    3. Introduction to Bridge Foundation 
  7. Bridge Bearing and Expansion joint
    (5 hours)
    1. Bridge bearing
      1. Types of bearing
      2. Design of metallic bearing
      3. Design of elastomeric bearing
      4. Expansion Joint
        1. Requirement to expansion joint
        2. Types of expansion joint and their design
  8. Construction and Maintenance of Bridge
    (4 hours)
    1. Introduction to construction of bridges
    2. Introduction to maintenance of bridges

Tutorial:

  2. Exercise on the application of Courbon's Method, Distribution Coefficient Method and Hendry Jaeger Method

  3. Exercise on the Design of T-Beam bridge
    (2 hours)
  4. Exercise on the Design of Composite Bridge
    (2 hours)

    (2 hours)
  6. Exercise on the Design of Bearing
    (2 hours)
  7. Exercise on the Design of Pier and Abutment
    (2.5 hours)

Practical:

Practical of the course consists of a minor project work and field work.

  1. Every individual student is assigned with a minor project work on design of RCC/ Steel Bridge and student has to defend the project work at the end of academic semester.
  2. One day field visit to bridge sites is organized. Student has to submit a visit report.
