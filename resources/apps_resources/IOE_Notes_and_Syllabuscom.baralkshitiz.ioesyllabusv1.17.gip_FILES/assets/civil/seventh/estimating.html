Course Objective:

To provide basic knowledge of estimating and costing of civil engineering works; to analyze the rates and estimate the cost of various construction works.

  1. Introduction
    (3 hours)
    1. Definition
    2. Estimated Cost and Actual Cost
    3. Purpose of Estimating
    4. Principle of Units and Measurement
    5. Units of Measurement and Payment for Various Items of Works and Materials
    6. Data Required for Estimating
  2. Method of Estimating
    (5 hours)
    1. Method of Measurements of Building and Civil Engineering Works
    2. Subheads of Various Items of Works
    3. Various Methods of Taking Out Quantities
    4. Abstracting Bill of Quantities
    5. Preparation of Detailed Estimate: Cost of Items, Contingencies Work charged Establishment
  3. Types of Estimate
    (3 hours)
    1. Approximate Estimates
    2. Detailed Estimate
    3. Revised Estimate
    4. Supplementary Estimate
    5. Annual Repair and Maintenance Estimate
    6. Extension and Improvement Estimate
    7. Complete Estimate
    8. Split Up of Cost of Building and Road WorksWater Supply and Sanitary Works.
  4. Analysis of Rates
    (9 hours)
    1. Introduction
    2. Purpose of Rate Analysis
    3. Importance of Rate Analysis
    4. Requirement of Rate Analysis
    5. Factors Affecting the Rate Analysis
    6. Method of Preparing Rate Analysis for
      1. building works
      2. road works
      3. sanitary and water supply works
      4. irrigation works
  5. Project Estimate
    (5 hours)
    1. Estimate for a Project

    3. Estimate for Building Project

    5. Estimate for Irrigation Project

    7. Estimate for Water Supply Project

    3. Estimate of Earth Work in Road Construction in Plain Area
    4. Estimate of Earth Work in Road Construction in Hilly Area
    5. Estimate of Earth Work in Canals
    6. Estimate for the Construction of Highway for One km Length
