TRAIL SUSPENSION BRIDGE
CE 72503

Lecture   : 3
Tutorial   : 1
Practical : 3/2

Course Objectives:

  • To introduce trail suspension bridge
  • To make capable to plan, analyse, design and construct trail suspension bridge
  1. Introduction
    (2 hours)
    1. Historical Background
    2. Trail Bridge in Nepal
    3. Classification of Trail Bridges and their Components
  2. Essential Data for Design and their Acquisition
    (6 hours)
    1. Essential Data for Design
    2. Socio-Economic Study
    3. Topographic Study and Engineering Survey
    4. Geological and Geotechnical Study
  3. Design of Trail Suspension Bridge
    (26 hours)
    1. Cable
      1. Introduction to Cable and its Specifications
      2. Cable Geometry and its Analytical Presentation
      3. Deformation of Cable and its Calculation
      4. Nonlinear Analysis of Extensible Cable
    2. Design of Main Cable Structure
      1. Cable Structure Design of Suspended Bridges
      2. Cable Structure Design of Suspension Bridges
    3. Design of Wind Bracing Structure
      1. Wind Guy Cable Arrangement
      2. Design of Wind Guy Structure
    4. Design of Suspenders/Hangers
    5. Design of Towers
    6. Design of Tower Foundation and Cable Anchorage
    7. Design Calculation of Trail Suspension Bridge by Computer
  4. Estimating and Costing
    (2 hours)
    1. Rate Analysis
    2. Cost Estimate
  5. Construction of Trail Suspension Bridge
    (9 hours)
    1. Construction Planning
    2. Setting Out of the Bridge
    3. Transportation, Handling and Hoisting of Cable
    4. Fabrication and Erection/Construction of Bridge
    5. Test Operation and Commissioning
    6. Maintenance of Bridge

Tutorial:

  1. Design Exercise on Cable Structure of N and D Type Bridges
    (8 hours)
  2. Design Exercise on Tower
    (4 hours)
  3. Design Exercise on Tower Foundation and Cable Anchorage
    (3 hours)

Practical:

  1. Practical of the course consists of a minor project work and field work.
  2. Every individual student is assigned with a minor project work on design of D / N type Trail Bridge and student has to defend the project work at the end of academic semester.
  3. One day field visit to bridge sites is organised. Student has to submit a visit report.
