ROPEWAY ENGINEERING
CE 72510

Lecture   : 3
Tutorial   : 1
Practical : 3/2

Course Objectives:

  • To introduce ropeway transport, types of ropeway and their components.
  • To make acquainted with the planning, analysis, design and construction of aerial and surface ropeway for passengers.
  • To make capable to plan, analyse, design and construct gravity ropeway for goods.
  1. Introduction
    (2 hours)
    1. Historical Background of Ropeway Transport
    2. Ropeway in Nepal
  2. Types of Ropeways and their Components
    (3 hours)
    1. Types of Ropeways
    2. Surface Ropeway for Passengers
    3. Aerial Ropeway for Passengers and Goods
    4. Gravity Ropeway for Goods
  3. Socio-Economic and Technical Study
    (5 hours)
    1. Pre-Feasibility Study
    2. Socio-Economic Study
    3. Topographic Study and Engineering Survey
    4. Geological and Geotechnical Study
  4. Design of Ropeway System
    (20 hours)
    1. General Design requirements and Design provisions
    2. Wire-Rope Design
      1. Introduction to Wire-Ropes and their Specifications
      2. Loads on Wire-Rope
      3. Wire-Rope Geometry
      4. Deformation of Wire-Rope and its Calculation
      5. Nonlinear Behavior of Wire-Rope and its Analysis and Design
    3. Design of Towers
    4. Design of Tower Foundation and Wire-Rope Anchorage
    5. Introduction to Electro-Mechanical System Design
  5. Estimating and Costing
    (2 hours)
    1. Rate Analysis
    2. Cost Estimate
  6. Planning, Construction and Maintenance of Ropeway System
    (9 hours)
    1. Construction Planning
    2. Setting Out
    3. Construction Equipment
    4. Transportation, Handling and Hoisting of Wire-Rope
    5. Construction, Installation and Maintenance
    6. Test Operation and Commissioning
  7. Quality Control and Safety
    (4 hours)
    1. Material Testing
    2. Safety Measures

Tutorial:

  1. Design exercise on wire-rope structure of aerial ropeway (6 hours)
  2. Design exercise on wire-rope anchorage and tower foundation on soil and rock (4 hours)
  3. Design exercise on tower structure (3 hours)
  4. Estimating and costing of gravity goods ropeway (2 hours)

Project work:

Design of a gravity ropeway system.

Field work:
Two days visit to ropeway sites, fabricators' workshops, implementing agencies of ropeway and submission of a report.
