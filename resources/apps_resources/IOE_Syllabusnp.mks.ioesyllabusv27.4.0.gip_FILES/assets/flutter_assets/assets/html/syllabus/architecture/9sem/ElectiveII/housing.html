Syllabus

HOUSING
AR 825 01

Course objective:

  1. To provide the students with the basic theoretical knowledge of housing .
  2. To make them acquainted with the current practice of housing in Nepal.
  3. To make them able to deal with  housing schemes.
  1. General and definition of housing (2 hours)
    1. General introduction
    2. Definition

  2. Theory of housing (4 hours)
    1. Structuralism
    2. Functionalism
    3. Formalism
    4. Economy theory

  3. Components of housing (4 hours)
    1. Physical components
    2. Administrative and regulatory components
    3. Management (construction)components
    4. Financial /Capital components
    5. Socio-economic components
    6. Cultural components

  4. Housing Standard (2 hours)
    1.  Physical indicator
    2.  Social indicator
    3.  Space standard
    4.  Changes of housing stock of various standards

  5. Socio -economic benefits of housing (4 hours)
    1. Status
    2. Infrastructure
    3. Security and satisfaction
    4. Economic Benefits

  6.  Shelter policy of Nepal (4 hours)
    1. Increasing the production of dwelling units and repair and maintenance of the existing stock.
    2. Promote effective mobilization and allocation of financial resources.
    3. Organizational improvement.

  7. Approaches of Housing delivery (4 hours)
    1. Government approach
    2. Popular/owner built housing approach
    3. Privative sector developer housing approach
    4. Cooperative approaches
    5. Community housing approach

  8. Parameters (2 hours)
    1. Physical parameters
    2. Economics parameters
    3. Social parameters
    4. Institutional parameters
  9. Apartment Act (4 hours)

Reference:

  1. Abrams, C., Housing in the Modern World: Man’s Struggle for Shelter in an Urbanizing World, MIT Press, Massachusetts
  2. Hamdi, N., Housing Without Houses: Participation, Flexibility, Enablement, London, Intermediate Technology Publications
  3. Hasan, A., Urban Housing Policies and Approaches in a Changing Asian Context, Karachi, City Press
  4. Joshi, J., Housing and Urban Development in Nepal: Analysis and Design of a Participatory Process, Kathmandu, Lajmina Joshi
  5. Sharma, K., Housing Finance in India, N. Delhi, Centre for Urban Studies, Indian Institute for Public Administration
  6. Turner, J.F.C, Housing by the People, N. York, Pantheon Books
