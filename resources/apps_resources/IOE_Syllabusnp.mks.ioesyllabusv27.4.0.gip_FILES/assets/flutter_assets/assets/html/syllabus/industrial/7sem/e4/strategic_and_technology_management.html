Syllabus

STRATEGIC AND TECHNOLOGY MANAGEMENT

Course objective

  1. Management information system (8 hours)
    1. Introduction and background frame work, information needed economics System view, role of MIS on various levels, structure of MIS, Information net work, system life cycle, data flow, decision trees.

  2. Corporate strategy and planning (6 hours)
    1. Concept of frame work, corporate management, role, function skill.

  3. Strategic analysis (8 hours)
    1. cost dynamics, portfolio analysis, financial analysis, Strategic choices. Alternating, diversification, mergers and acquisition implementation and evaluation of strategy.

  4. Strategic management and leadership (8 hours)
    1. Role of leadership, process of leadership, line structure, styles.

  5. Technology management (8 hours)
    1. Technology life cycle , transformation, alternatives, appropriate technology, technology change, technology transfer, models.

  6. Technology absorption: Assessment (6 hours)
    1. evaluation, diffusion.
