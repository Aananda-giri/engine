WORKING DRAWING

Course Objective:
  1. After the completion of this course the students will be able to make complete set of working drawings.
  2. The students will make one complete set of manual working drawings of a 3 storey residential building of approx.1000Sq.Ft.plinth area of their own design completed in previous design Studio classes.
