Syllabus

Aviation Legislation

Course Objectives:
To have brief understanding of Aviation Regulation followed around the world

  1. Regulatory Framework (15 Hours)
    1. Introduction
      1. International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO), International Air Transport Association (IATA) and Its roles
      2. The Convention of ICAO
      3. ICAO Annexes
      4. Role of Civil Aviation Authority of Nepal
      5. Civil Aviation Act 1959 and subsequent amendments
      6. Civil Aviation Authority Act 1996 and subsequent amendments
      7. Civil Aviation Rules 1996 and subsequent amendments
      8. Civil Aviation Authority Regulation 2002 and subsequent amendments
      9. General Introduction FAA and EASA
    2. Certifying Staff
      1. Understanding of EASA Part 66
      2. Detailed Understanding of NCAR Part 66
    3. Approved Maintenance Organization
      1. Understanding of EASA Part M subpart F and Part 145
      2. Detail Understanding of NCAR Part 145 and NCAR Part M

  2. Operation- Commercial Air Transportation (10 Hours)
    1. General Understanding of EU-Operations
    2. Air Operator Certificates and its requirement
    3. Operator Responsibilities- CAMO and AMO
    4. Aircraft Maintenance Program (AMP)
    5. Minimum Equipment List and Configuration Deviation List (MEL/CDL)
    6. Documents to be carried on board the aircraft
    7. Aircraft Placarding (Marking) Requirement

  3. Aircraft Certification (8 Hours)
    1. General
      1. Design Organization Part 21 (EASA and NCAR)
      2. Certification Rules: Type Certificates and Supplemental Type Certificates
    2. Documents
      1. Certificate of Airworthiness
      2. Certificate of Registration
      3. Noise Certificate
      4. Weight Schedule
      5. Radio Station License

  4. Applicable National and International Requirement (12 Hour)
    1. Maintenance Program
    2. Maintenance Checks and Inspection
    3. Airworthiness Directives
    4. Service Bulletins and Manufacturers Service Information
    5. Modification and Repair
    6. Maintenance Documents: Manuals
    7. MMEL, DDL
    8. Continuing Airworthiness- Test Flight
    9. Category 2/3 Operation, All Weather Operation
    10. ETOPS, Maintenance and Dispatch Requirements

Practical:

  1. Write report on various topics on aviation legislation.
  2. Create short report and presentation on understanding of aviation legislation issues.

References:

  1. Nepal Civil Aviation Regulation
  2. EASA Part M, Part 145, Part 21, Part 66
  3. ICAO Annex 1-19
  4. IATA
  5. NCAR Part 66, 145,147
  6. FAA Airworthiness Directives
  7. Transport Canada
  8. Bombardier Service Manual
  9. Boeing Online Technical Publications
