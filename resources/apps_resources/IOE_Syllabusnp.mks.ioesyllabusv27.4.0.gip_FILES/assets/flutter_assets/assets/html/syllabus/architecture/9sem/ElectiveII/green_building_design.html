Syllabus

GREEN BUILDING DESIGN
AR 825 03

Course Objective:

  1. To introduce the basic concepts of Green building in relation to architectural design
  2. To deal with the use of the Green concept in design and construction to produce healthy, economical, comfortable, energy efficient and environment friendly green buildings.
  1. Concept of Green Building [4 hours]
    1. Introduction and Objectives
    2. History of Green Building
    3. Importance of Green Building
    4. Concept of Green Building

  2. Green Building Rating System [2 hours]
    1. Building Rating System
    2. LEED, BREEAM, CASBEE, GRIHA etc.

  3. Green Building Design [4 hours]
    1. Site efficiency
    2. Green material and resources
    3. Green technology
    4. Energy efficiency
    5. Water efficiency
    6. Indoor air quality
    7. Waste management
    8. Innovative design

  4. Site Efficiency [2 hours]
    1. Site – Orientation, landscape, open space
    2. Site – Transportation, community service

  5. Green Building Material and resources [3 hours]
    1. Embodied energy of material
    2. Reuse & recycle material
    3. Healthy environment friendly material – CSEB, Green concrete, VSBK brick etc

  6. Green Building Technology [3 hours]
    1. Traditional technique – wall, floor, roof, Rammed earth
    2. Modern Green technique - Rat-trap bond, cavity structure, Inco- panel, UPVC door, windows, CSEB roof etc.

  7. Energy Efficiency [5 hours]
    1. Energy efficient Lighting – Day lighting, artificial lighting
    2. Day lighting – Atrium, Sky light
    3. Artificial lighting – CFL, LED lighting,
    4. Passive Solar Design
      Passive solar heating, Passive solar cooling, Passive solar heating & cooling
      1. Passive solar heating - Direct, Indirect, Isolated gain, color, texture
      2. Passive solar cooling – Shading, ventilation, color, texture Courtyard effect, Air cooling, Earth cooling, EAT, Evaporation cooling
      3. Combined Passive heating and cooling
      4. Trombe wall, Roof ponds, Solar chimney, Wind tower, etc.
      5. Active system- Solar PV panel, Solar water heater

  8. Water Efficiency, Solid waste management & Indoor air quality [1 hour]
    1. Water-efficient toilet, Sensor taps
    2. Rainwater harvesting, Waste water treatment
    3. Biomass - Biogas, bio-digester

  9. Case study of Green Buildings in Nepal [2 hours]
    1. Zero Energy Building (CES)
    2. Green School Buildings
    3. LEED rated Buildings

  10. Project work with the application Green Building Design [4 hours]
    1. Design of a Green building in Nepal
    2. Climatic data- Solar radiation, Temperature, Wind pattern, Humidity, Precipitation
    3. 10.3  Micro-climatic data- local wind flow, Solar access to the site, vegetation, water body
    4. Objective – Design response with green, energy efficiency & environment friendly
    5. Site selection - with transportation, community service, environment  friendly
    6. Application of energy-efficient design
    7. Selection of Green materials & construction technology
    8. Integration of Renewable Energy systems
    9. Lighting & HVAC system
    10. Water efficiency & Solid waste management
    11. Conclusion
