Syllabus

COMMUNICATIONS (English & Nepali)

ENGLISH

Course Objective
To make the students capable of producing professional writings such at technical proposals, reports and project works     

Unit I: Reading

  1. Intensive Reading (5 hours)
    1. Comprehension
    2. Note-taking
    3. Summary Writing
    4. Contextual questions based on facts and imagination
    5. Interpreting texts

  2. Extensive Reading (3 hours)
    1. Title/Topic Speculation
    2. finding theme
    3. Sketching character

  3. Contextual Grammar (2 hours)
    1. Sequence of tense
    2. Voice
    3. subject-Verb agreement
    4. Conditional Sentences
    5. Preposition

Unit II

  1. Varieties of English (5 hours)
    1. Formal/Informal English
    2. Familiar/Polite/More Polite English
    3. Personal/Impersonal English

Unit III

  1. Writing Technical Proposal (4 hours)
    1. Introduction
    2. Parts of the proposal
      1. title page
      2. Abstract/Summary
      3. Statement of problem
      4. Rational
      5. Objectives
      6. Procedure/Methodology
      7. Cost estimate or Budget
      8. Time management/schedule
      9. Summary
      10. Conclusion
      11. References
      12. Annexes

Unit IV

  1. Writing formal Reports (6 hours)
    1. Formal Reports
      1. Introduction
      2. Parts and components of formal Report
        1. Preliminary Section
          1. Cover page
          2. Letter of transmittal/Preface
          3. Title page
          4. Acknowledgements
          5. Table of figures and tables
          6. Abstract/Executive summary
        2. Main section
          1. Introduction
          2. Discussion/Body
          3. Summary/Conclusion
          4. Recommendations
        3. Documentation
          1. Notes (contextual/Footnotes)
          2. Bibliography
          3. Appendix

Unit V

  1. Writing Project Work (4 hours)
    1. Introduction
    2. Parts
