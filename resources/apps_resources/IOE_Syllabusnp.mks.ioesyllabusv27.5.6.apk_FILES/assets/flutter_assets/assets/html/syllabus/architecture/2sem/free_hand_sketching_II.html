Syllabus

FREE HAND SKETCHING-II

Course Objective:  To impart Knowledge & develop skill of free hand sketching with free hand perspective and basic exercise of color, pencil, pen, brush etc.

  1. Introduction to advanced free hand sketching skill (interior, exterior & landscape) (4 hours)
    1. Exercises of tone, shade & shadow by pencil, color, pen in different objects
  2. Free hand sketching of interior space(8 hours)
    1. Free hand sketching of interior space of historical environment by the following techniques
      1. Pencil
      2. Ink
      3. Color
    2. Free hand sketching of interior space of modern Architecture environment by
      1. Pencil
      2. Ink
      3. Color
  3. Free hand sketching of exterior space (8 hours)
    1. free hand sketching of exterior space of historical architecture environment by
      1. Pencil
      2. Ink
      3. Color
    2. Free hand sketching of exterior space of modern architecture environment by
      1. Pencil
      2. Ink
      3. Color
  4. Free hand fast sketching ( by color ) of different objects – human figures, tree, vehicles, furniture, perspective views, clouds, etc (16 hours)
  5. Free hand sketching of landscape by color(4 hours)
  6. Introduction of anatomy(8 hours) 
    1. Simple Pencil fast sketching of different parts of human figure (live & gypsum)
      1. Gypsum bodies (eye, lips, ear, nose, hand, foot, head etc.)
      2. Live full figure (male & female)
      3. Self portrait by pencil       
  7. Review, consultation & submission(4 hours)
