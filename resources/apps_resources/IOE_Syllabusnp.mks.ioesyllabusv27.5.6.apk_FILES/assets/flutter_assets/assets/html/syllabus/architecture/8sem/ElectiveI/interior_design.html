Syllabus

INTERIOR DESIGN
AR 765 05

Course Objective:

  1. To provide the students the theoretical knowledge of interior design
  2. To make them acquainted with the curre.. practice of interior design in different buildings
  3. To make them able to deal with various interior design & declination problems
  1. Introduction (2 hours)
    1. Interior spaces
    2. Interior design
    3. Interior Display
    4. Importance of architecture in Interiors
    5. The process of ID
    6. Design principles & elements

  2. Special Population (4 hours)
    1. Design for special population
    2.  The ADA and Universal population
    3.  Types of special population

  3. Colour (2 hours)
    1. Historical Evolution of colour
    2.  The standard colour wheel theory and scheme
    3.  Colour harmony and Properties of colours
    4.  The colour trend market

  4. Light (4 hours)
    1. Light in interiors
    2. Categories of Artificial Lighting
    3. Light controls and effect
    4. Metamerism

  5.  Space Planning And The Design Process(4 hours)
    1. Plan arrangement
    2. Traffic patterns
    3. Floor plan symbols

  6. Furniture Layout/Details And Human Factors (4 hours)
    1. Furniture as symbol and History
    2. Furniture arrangement and layout
    3. Design principles   in furnitures
    4. Human factors

  7. Architectural Details(4 hours)
    1. Architectural details in interiors
    2. Wall ceiling Doors and windows
    3. Treatment and finishes
    4. Other details

  8. Vastu In Interiors (4 hours)
    1. Introduction to Vaastu
      1. Vastu Purusha (Vedic Vastu)
      2. Vastu Mandala
      3. Five Elements and Scientific Application
    2. Planning
      1. Orientation (Space)
      2. Layout (Furniture / Fixtures)
      3. Practical vastu tips for internal environments
    3. Energy and Materials
      1. Energy from colour and light
      2. Vastu remedies in interiors

  9. Environmental Considerations (2 hours)
    1. Environmental considerations of different material used
    2. The affecting factors in interiors
